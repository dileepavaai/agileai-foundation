AGILE AI FOUNDATION — FORWARD GOVERNANCE PLANNING

Governance Evolution Roadmap

This document outlines potential governance refinements and structural evolution considerations for future advancement of the Agile AI Functional Elements framework.

The current canonical baseline remains Version 1.1. Future advancement may occur as a minor refinement (v1.2) or as a structural revision (v2.0). No modifications are currently active.

Scope Boundary

  • No change to the Six Functional Elements
  • No change to the Authority Model
  • No expansion of institutional positioning
  • No semantic alteration of canonical definitions

Any modification beyond these boundaries requires formal advancement to a major version (v2.0 or later).

Permitted Enhancements (Non-Structural Only)

  • Typography refinement
  • Reference formatting improvements
  • Semantic metadata enhancement
  • Archival integrity strengthening
  • Navigation clarity within governance pages

These refinements do not alter definitions, structure, authority, or canonical meaning.

Reserved for Structural Revision

  • Addition of new Functional Elements
  • Redefinition of existing elements
  • Authority restructuring
  • Structural expansion of the canonical model

Structural evolution, if required, will occur only through deliberate major version advancement.

Status: Planning Reference — No Active Changes
