Agile AI Foundation — Institutional Positioning

Institutional Positioning Statement

This statement clarifies the institutional role, neutrality boundaries, and constitutional positioning of the Agile AI Foundation.

The Agile AI Foundation defines structured governance frameworks for Agile AI Functional Elements.

Positioning Principles

  • Defines WHAT must exist for Agile AI systems
  • Does not define HOW implementation must occur
  • Does not issue certifications
  • Does not accredit providers
  • Does not operate as a commercial training entity

Institutional Neutrality

The Foundation maintains structural neutrality. It does not compete with educational institutions, training providers, consultancies, or certification bodies.

Its sole mandate is to preserve, govern, and version-control the canonical definition of Agile AI Functional Elements.
