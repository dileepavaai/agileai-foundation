Agile AI Foundation — Institutional Communication Governance

Institutional Contact Protocol

This protocol defines appropriate inquiry scope, communication boundaries, and governance-related contact discipline.

The Agile AI Foundation may be contacted for governance-related matters within the defined authority scope.

Light · Dark · System

Appropriate Inquiries

  • Academic citation clarification
  • Version and archival inquiries
  • Governance interpretation questions
  • Institutional collaboration discussions (non-commercial)

Outside Scope

  • Certification validation
  • Training accreditation
  • Commercial consulting requests
  • Tool endorsement inquiries

Requests falling outside the governance mandate will not be processed.

Official Contact Channel

support@agileai.university

All correspondence must clearly reference the applicable version (e.g., v1.0) and specify the governance context of the inquiry.
