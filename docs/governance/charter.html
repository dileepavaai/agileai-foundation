Agile AI Foundation — Constitutional Governance

Governance Charter

This charter defines the constitutional authority, structural boundaries, and version discipline governing the Agile AI Functional Elements.

The Agile AI Foundation exists to define, preserve, and govern the canonical definition of Agile AI Functional Elements.

Scope of Authority

  • Canonical definition of Functional Elements
  • Version discipline and archival control
  • Authority boundaries and structural integrity
  • Preservation of constitutional consistency across releases

Governance Model

Governance is centralized, version-controlled, and publicly documented. Structural changes require formal version advancement and explicit declaration.

Definitions remain stable within declared versions. Interpretations may evolve; canonical structure does not drift.

Institutional Independence

The Foundation does not provide training, certification, consulting, assessments, or commercial services.

Learning, interpretation, and applied frameworks may reference the Functional Elements, but they do not redefine them.
