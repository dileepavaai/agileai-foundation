Agile AI Foundation — Intellectual Property Governance

Intellectual Property & Usage Policy

This policy defines permitted usage, attribution requirements, and structural protection boundaries for Agile AI Functional Elements.

The Agile AI Functional Elements constitute original intellectual work governed by the Agile AI Foundation.

Permitted Uses

  • Academic citation and scholarly reference
  • Educational discussion and analysis
  • Alignment analysis and commentary
  • Non-commercial publication with proper attribution

Prohibited Uses

  • Rebranding or presenting the work as original authorship
  • Structural modification presented as canonical definition
  • Misrepresentation of certification or accreditation authority
  • Commercial misrepresentation of endorsement by the Foundation

Derivative Interpretations

Derivative interpretations must clearly state the following disclosure:

“This work interprets the Agile AI Functional Elements as defined by the Agile AI Foundation and does not modify the canonical definition.”

Derivative materials must not imply structural revision, certification authority, or endorsement unless explicitly granted.
