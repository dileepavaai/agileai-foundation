v1.1 Governance Roadmap (Planned)

This document outlines potential non-structural refinements for a future v1.1 release. No changes are active until formally versioned and declared. Governance authority remains centralized and controlled.

Version 1.0 of the Agile AI Functional Elements is structurally frozen. This roadmap does not introduce, redefine, or reinterpret the Six Functional Elements.

Scope Boundary

  • No change to the Six Functional Elements
  • No change to the Authority Model
  • No expansion of institutional or commercial positioning
  • No semantic alteration of canonical definitions

Any modification beyond these boundaries requires formal advancement to a major version (v2.0 or later).

Permitted Enhancements (Non-Structural Only)

  • Typography refinement
  • Reference formatting improvements
  • Semantic metadata enhancement
  • Archival integrity strengthening
  • Navigation clarity within governance pages

These enhancements do not alter definitions, structure, authority, or canonical meaning.

Reserved for v2.0 Only

  • Addition of new Functional Elements
  • Redefinition of existing elements
  • Authority restructuring
  • Structural expansion of the canonical model

Structural evolution, if ever required, will occur only through deliberate major version advancement.

Status: Planning Stage — Not Active
