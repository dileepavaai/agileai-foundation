Authority Model

The Agile AI Foundation defines and governs the Functional Elements. Training programs, derivative materials, assessments, or interpretive models do not redefine canonical elements.

Definitions change only through formal version advancement. Interpretations may evolve. Canonical structure does not drift silently.
