AGILE AI FOUNDATION

Agile AI Foundation defines structured frameworks, capability standards, and governance models for the Agile AI domain. It operates as an independent institutional authority dedicated to the development and stewardship of Agile AI standards.

Education and training related to Agile AI standards are provided independently through Agile AI University and its partner organizations, including licensed and accredited trainers.
