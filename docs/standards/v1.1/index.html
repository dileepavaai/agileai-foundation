The Six Agile AI Functional Elements ¶

Intent & Outcome Alignment ¶

Agile AI systems must be explicitly aligned to clear intent and desired outcomes.

Human Judgment & Accountability ¶

Human authority remains responsible for direction, constraints, and ethical boundaries governing AI-enabled systems.

Adaptive Execution ¶

Agile AI systems must continuously adapt based on feedback and environmental signals.

Continuous Learning & Feedback ¶

Systems must learn continuously from outcomes and operational experience.

Governance by Design ¶

Governance must be intentionally embedded within Agile AI systems.

Systemic Integration ¶

Agile AI operates within broader socio-technical systems.
