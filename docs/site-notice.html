About This Site

The Agile AI Foundation website exists to preserve and publish a clear, stable, and neutral definition of Agile AI Functional Elements.

This site is intentionally static. It does not provide training, certification, assessments, consulting, or commercial services. Learning and application resources are maintained separately.

Usage data, if collected, is anonymous, aggregated, and privacy-respecting. No personal data is stored, profiled, or sold.

Changes to the published definitions are rare, deliberate, and versioned. The English version of the content is the authoritative reference.
