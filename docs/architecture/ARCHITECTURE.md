# Agile AI Ecosystem Architecture

This document explains the structural architecture of the Agile AI ecosystem.

Agile AI is positioned as a **structured reference model for AI-enabled operating systems** rather than a prescriptive framework or methodology.

The ecosystem maintained by the Agile AI Foundation is organized into multiple conceptual and structural layers that together support the evolution, governance, and adoption of Agile AI.

---

# 1. Architectural Overview

The Agile AI ecosystem is composed of the following layers:

Concept  
Definition  
Specification  
Standards Registry  
Governance  
Adoption  
Capability Development

Each layer serves a specific purpose in maintaining the integrity and evolution of the Agile AI reference model.

---

The concept layer defines the foundational idea of Agile AI.

# 10. Architectural Principles

The Agile AI ecosystem is guided by several architectural principles.

Conceptual Integrity  
The definition and meaning of Agile AI must remain consistent across all artifacts.

Structural Clarity  
The ecosystem must clearly separate conceptual, specification, governance, and adoption layers.

Institutional Stewardship  
The Agile AI Foundation maintains the canonical concept and standards.

Framework Neutrality  
Agile AI does not prescribe a specific development framework.

Instead, it provides a structural reference model that can operate alongside existing delivery approaches.

---

# Conclusion

Agile AI is not a single document or framework.

It is an evolving ecosystem of concepts, definitions, specifications, governance mechanisms, and adoption guidance maintained by the Agile AI Foundation.

This architectural structure ensures that Agile AI can grow as a durable and institutionally governed reference model for AI-enabled systems.
