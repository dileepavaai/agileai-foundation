AGILE AI FOUNDATION — CONCEPT DEFINITION

Agile AI

Agile AI is a structural reference model for the design, development, and operation of AI-enabled systems that deliver the right outcomes at the right time through the coordinated application of Agile practices, artificial intelligence capabilities, and human judgment supported by continuous capability development.

The Agile AI model focuses on improving how organizations design, build, and operate AI-enabled systems so that they remain adaptive, reliable, transparent, and responsibly governed.

The structural elements required for Agile AI systems are formally defined in the Agile AI Functional Elements Standard maintained by the Agile AI Foundation.
