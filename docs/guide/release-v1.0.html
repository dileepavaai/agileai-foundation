  • The structural definition of Agile AI remains fixed within v1.0.
  • Interpretive guidance does not constitute structural mutation.
  • No silent modification is permitted.
  • Any future structural evolution requires formal version increment and archival preservation.

This document is issued as an archival publication and reflects the authoritative interpretive posture of the Foundation as of 27 February 2026.
