Agile AI

Agile AI provides the structural foundation for adaptive, intelligent, and responsibly governed AI systems.

Agile AI = Adaptive execution + machine intelligence + accountable human judgment

Status: Canonical Institutional Standard · Version: 1.1 · Release Year: 2026

This specification represents the current canonical release of the Agile AI Functional Elements and is maintained by the Agile AI Foundation under formal institutional governance and version discipline.

Agile AI is a structural reference model for the design, development, and operation of AI-enabled systems that deliver the right outcomes at the right time through the coordinated application of Agile practices, artificial intelligence capabilities, and human judgment supported by continuous capability development.

The Agile AI Foundation defines and governs the canonical structural standards that establish the essential Functional Elements required for responsible and adaptive AI-enabled systems.

Institutional Positioning

The Agile AI Foundation operates as an independent academic and professional body that defines structured frameworks and canonical standards for the Agile AI domain.

The Foundation does not provide training or certification. Educational programs and applied initiatives may interpret the standard but do not redefine it.
