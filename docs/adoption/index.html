AGILE AI FOUNDATION — STANDARD ADOPTION

Adopting the Agile AI Standard

Organizations may voluntarily align their AI-enabled systems, operational practices, or governance structures with the Agile AI Functional Elements standard published by the Agile AI Foundation.

Adoption of the standard allows organizations to structure their AI initiatives around the canonical functional elements defined in the Agile AI specification.

Adoption Statement

Organizations that align with the standard may publicly state:

"This organization aligns its AI-enabled systems with the Agile AI Functional Elements v1.1 published by the Agile AI Foundation."

Important Clarification

Adoption of the Agile AI standard does not imply certification, formal endorsement, or audit by the Agile AI Foundation.

The foundation publishes the canonical structural specification and maintains version discipline for the standard. Implementation and interpretation remain the responsibility of adopting organizations.
